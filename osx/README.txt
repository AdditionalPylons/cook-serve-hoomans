Cook, Serve, Hoomans!
=====================

To apply this mod just double click cook_serve_hoomans.command. If that gives
you and error and can't find the Cook, Serve, Delicious! installation double
click open_with_cook_serve_hoomans.command and manually choose the game archive.

Usually the game archive is called "game.ios" ("data.win" on Windows and
"game.unx" on Linux) and located somewhere in you Cook, Serve, Delicious!
installation directory.
